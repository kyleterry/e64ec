I flew to London for a couple days, then headed to Berlin for the next week. I'd
never been to either of those places before. London was okay, but it felt very
touristy (yes, I know I'm one of them). Berlin, on the other hand, was awesome.
It felt very calm, the public transit system was amazing (US struggles with this
obviously), and it had all the vegan options a boy like me can ever want.
