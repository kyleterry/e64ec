Hello! I drew up a new site logo. I chose a metal-inspired theme. I think the next one I draw will be more of a slam metal design, but I’ve been interested in some of the european black metal logos that don’t incorporate too many drips or symmetry.

I drew it up in Procreate. Most things I draw start in Procreate and usually make their way to cold-press paper sketches. I really enjoy watercolor painting and I promise to share more of my stuff here. You can see some of it spread around the pages as banners.
